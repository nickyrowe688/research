


Ha ty, pryv, a wra kreupya, 	And thou, Serpent, shalt creep,
cwms0913 ha slynckya war doer a heys 	ha slynkya war dor a-hys.


Some prepositions in English must be translated into Cornish using adverbs which follow a prepositional phrase:

- along
  -  ha te preif a wra cruppya |  ha slynckya **war doer a heys** (CW912-3) <br /> ha ty, pryv, a wra kreupya | ha slynkya **war dor a-hys** <br /> and thou, serpent, shall creep and slink **along the ground**
  - me a vyn dallath cruppya |  ha slyncya **war doer a heys** (CW923-4) <br /> my a vynn dalleth kreupya | ha slynkya **war dor a-hys** <br /> I will begin to creep and slink **along the ground**
