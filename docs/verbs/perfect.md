# Perfect

## Periphrastic perfect

Uses bos short form to express a state

- Kodhys yw an fosow

A subject is expressed with gans, which in turn triggers the use of the long form, as it is interpreted as a possessive construction:

- Yma gwelys genen steren y'n est
- Eus gweles genes ow hi?
- Eus konys dhywgh?
- 
- leb ma leverres gennam a orama dro tho an tavaz Curnooack (NB) "where I have said what I know about the Cornish language"
