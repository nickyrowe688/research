Defective verbs

heveli

Present

  • it seems to sb
    • Ny amownt thymma resna genas, Noy, me a hevall (2396) "It is no good for me to reason with you, Noah, it seems to me"
    • Hag in y devra flogh teake, der havall thym indella (1837) "And in her arms a beautiful child, so it seems to me"
    • Hevol dem kallish (NB) "it seems hard to me"
    • mar dewethaz, kar dre hevol, thera Curnoock en pow na (NB) "so recently, so it seems, was Cornish in that district"

Imperfect

  • it seems to sb
    • My a fylly in urna a callen dos than pryckna (784) "It seemed to me at that time that if I might achieve that..."

Conditional

  • it seems to sb
    • Marow seigh hy a valsa (1810) "It would appear to be dead dry" (cf Breton skuizh maro "dead tired")
    • Rag der tha ere yth falsa ty tha thos an Nev totheta (566) "For from what you say it would appear that you came straight from Heaven"
    • Yth falsa orth y favoure y bosa neb bucka noos (1586) "It would appear from the look of it that it is some sort of goblin"

res

Present

  • must
    • Why reze lawle tha guz tâz ha damma (Gw) "You must say to your father and mother"
    • Mee reze moze tha Loundres (Gw) "I must go to London"
    • Why rez cawse dha guz tâz ha guz damma (Gw) "You must talk to your father and mother"
    • Reiz yụ dhebm (L) "I must"
    • Res ew sawya an pyth es (2443) "one must save what there is"
    • Me an gorth del ryes (187) "I will worship him as one ought"
    • Ny ryse thewh ow mystrustya (677) "You ought not to mistrust me"
    • Na reys gara an vor goth rag an vor noweth (WS) "One must not leave the old road for the new road"
    • Reys yu, purryes, lavyrrya (1077) "It is necessary, most necessary, to labour"
    • Nyngew reis skemyna moye (1212) "There is no need to do any more banning"

Imperfect

  • had to
    • Reiz ô dhyz (L) "You had to"

Future

  • will have to
    • Biz reiz dhodho (L) "He will have to"

Subjunctive

  • may have to
    • pen vo reys degevy (1068) "when the need arises to pay the tithe"
    • Po res dala'n vor na oren pana tu (P) "When it is necessary to begin the journey we do not know which way..."

talves / talveja

Present

  • Supposition
    • Eve a dael bose Ankou e hunnen (NB) "it must be Death himself"
    • Eve a dale bose gen kine eu ginnez ubba (NB) "It must be by others than have been born here"
  • Prohibition
    • Na dale ni gurra ul tha gotha (JT) "We must not let everything go"
    • Ny dâl dhẏnny (L) "We must not"
    • Ny dâl dhodho (L) "He must not"
    • Nyn dale thym towlall the veas... (2444) "I must not waste"
  • Exhortation
    • Na dale deiw gwell treaven war an treath (J) "You ought not to build houses on the sand"
    • Na dale deez perna kinnis war an sawe (J) "You ought not to buy fuel by the horse-load"
    • Nag es travith dale talves an bees, Bus gen Dieu benegas do gweel gan crees (NB) "There is nothing the world ought to value, Except to make our peace with God the blessed"
  • Necessity/obligation
    • Me dâl gụellaz an ôst an tshei (L/NB) "I must see the landlord"
  • Normal meaning of 'be worth'
    • Môr-teed a metten travyth ne dâl (Gw) "The morning tide is worth nothing"
    • Rewhy ran thagas dremas, po an vyadge ny dale tra (681) "Give some to your goodman, or the concern will not be worth anything"
    • mar crown gy predery pan dâl go gwary (J) "If they stop to think what their games are worth"

Future

  • Be worth
    • Oll agen vyadge ny ny dalvyth oye (2065) "All our venture will not be worth an egg"

Conditional

  • Should
    • Et eye ollas hye dalveha gowas tane (J) "In her hearth she should have a fire"
    • Why dalveha gowas an brossa mine (J) "You should get the largest stones"

kodha

Present

  • I ought, it behoves
    • Me a goth in pur thefrye gorthya Dew (1956) "I ought most certainly to worship God"
    • Me a levar thewh, dell goeth, an gwreanathe a bub tra (1891) "I will tell you, as is fitting, the truth of everything"
    • Ha rag henna y coth thyma gans colan pure aga gwerthya (1964) "And for that reason I ought with a pure heart to worship them"
    • Peb am honor pardel goyth (1438) "Everyone honours me as is right"

Past subjunctive (Imperative?)

  • Should
    • Gwayte ow gworria war bub tewe, zeso ge pardel gotha (49) "Take care to worship me, as you should"
    • Eve: Ow threst yw y vos da. Serpent: Da cotha! (583) "Eve: I trust that it is good. Serpent: It should be good!"
    • Mar am kerowgh dell gotha (2526) "If you love me as one should"
    • E brederaz ter gotha dhodho bɐz avizyiz dhiụeth ken gụesgal enneth (L/NB) "He remembered that he should be warned twice before striking once"
    • Pẏ gotha dhyz bɐz dhyz levar (L) "When you should be at your book"

Conditional

  • would have to
    • Po 'rẏg an dzhei dhɐz dhɐ'n tshei lebma gothfîa an dzhei ostia (L/NB) "When they reached the house where they would have to stay"
    • Na ellam ry dotha mouy tre guffia them (NB) "I cannot accord it more than I should/would have to"
